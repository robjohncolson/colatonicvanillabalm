Colatonic Vanilla Balm

A Coca-Cola Inspired Beard Care Innovation

About

This recipe provides detailed instructions for crafting two complementary beard care products: a Beard Tonic and a Vanilla Beard Balm. Inspired by the iconic scent profile of Coca-Cola, these products work together to create a unique grooming experience. The tonic offers a light, fragrant conditioning effect, while the balm provides hold, moisture, and a deeper, resinous sweetness.

Beard Tonic Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Purpose
Frankincense Resin 6 grams Adds warm, balsamic depth and a resinous texture for light hold
Myrrh Resin 3 grams Contributes earthy, slightly sweet notes and enhances shaping
Glycerin 250 microliters (0.25 ml) Acts as a humectant to retain moisture and help dissolve resins
Ethanol (95% or higher) ~5.7 ml (adjust to 15 ml total) Dissolves resins and essences; acts as a preservative
Violet Leaf Absolute 14.39 microliters Provides a fresh, green, sparkling note to mimic cola's effervescence
Bergamot Essential Oil 300 microliters Adds a bright, citrusy top note, echoing cola's orange and lime hints
Neroli Essential Oil 100 microliters Contributes a floral, slightly citrusy middle note for complexity
Coriander Essential Oil 200 microliters Offers a spicy, citrusy middle note, aligning with cola's spice blend
Ceylon Cinnamon Essential Oil 50 microliters Provides a warm, spicy note reminiscent of cola's cinnamon undertone
Nutmeg Absolute 100 microliters Delivers a rich, warm, spicy base note for depth
Lime Essential Oil (Expressed) 50 microliters Adds a zesty, citrusy top note to enhance the cola-inspired freshness
Ginger CO2 Extract 10-15 microliters Introduces a subtle, warm spice whisper for complexity
Lemon Essential Oil (Organic) 50 microliters Adds a crisp, bright citrus note to complete the cola citrus trio

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Resins: Grind 6 grams of frankincense and 3 grams of myrrh into a coarse powder using a mortar and pestle.
  2. Mix Resins with Glycerin: Place the ground resins into a 15 ml glass bottle. Add 250 microliters of glycerin and shake well.
  3. Prepare the Essence Mixture: Combine all essential oils and absolutes in a small container using pipettes.
  4. Combine with Ethanol: Add ~5.7 ml of ethanol to the essence mixture, adjusting to ensure total liquid volume is ~6.764 ml.
  5. Add to Resins: Pour the ethanol-essence mixture into the bottle with resins and glycerin.
  6. Steep and Shake: Cap the bottle and shake vigorously. Let steep for 24 hours, shaking occasionally.
  7. Optional Filtration: Filter through fine mesh or cheesecloth for a clearer tonic.

Vanilla Beard Balm Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Purpose
Carnauba Wax 7 grams Provides firm hold and heat stability
Kokum Butter 7 grams Delivers deep conditioning with a non-greasy finish
Squalane (Olive-derived) 10 grams Acts as a lightweight, odorless carrier
Vanilla Absolute 120 microliters Supplies the sweet, creamy backbone of the scent
Frankincense Resin 1 gram (ground fine) Adds warm, resinous depth and slight tackiness
Myrrh Resin 0.5 gram (ground fine) Contributes earthy warmth
Orange Blossom Absolute 50 microliters Introduces a floral, sweet-citrus middle note
Tonka Bean 20% Absolute 50 microliters Adds a sweet, vanilla-like warmth
Benzoin Resinoid 50 microliters Provides a balsamic, sweet base note
Cardamom CO2 Extract 10-15 microliters Offers a subtle, warm spice whisper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Resins: Grind 1 gram of frankincense and 0.5 gram of myrrh into a fine powder.
  2. Melt the Base: In a double boiler, combine carnauba wax, kokum butter, and squalane. Heat until fully melted.
  3. Add Resins and Essences: Stir in ground resins and all essences thoroughly.
  4. Pour and Cool: Remove from heat and pour into a 30 ml tin. Let cool until solid (1-2 hours).

Using the Products Together

  1. Apply the Beard Tonic First: Shake well and apply a few drops to your beard. Massage in for light conditioning.
  2. Follow with the Vanilla Beard Balm: Warm a small amount between your fingers and work it through your beard.

Scent Profile

Tonic

The violet leaf, citrus (lime, bergamot, lemon), and spices (ginger, coriander, cinnamon, nutmeg) create a fresh, sparkling effect reminiscent of cola's initial fizz and citrus-spice burst.

Balm

Vanilla, orange blossom, tonka, benzoin, and cardamom add a warm, resinous sweetness that echoes cola's deeper, caramel-like undertones.

Safety Notes

⚠️ Important Safety Information:

  • For external use only
  • Keep away from eyes and mucous membranes
  • Discontinue use if irritation occurs
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep out of reach of children
