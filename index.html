Colatonic Vanilla Balm

A Coca-Cola Inspired Beard Care Innovation

About

Colatonic Vanilla Balm is a unique beard care product that combines the iconic flavors of Coca-Cola with the nourishing properties of frankincense and vanilla. This innovative formula provides a refreshing and aromatic experience while maintaining your beard's health and appearance.

Ingredients

Frankincense

Natural resin with anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties

Vanilla

Provides a sweet, comforting aroma and natural conditioning

Coca-Cola Essence

Adds the signature refreshing cola scent

Natural Oils

Blend of nourishing oils for optimal beard health

Usage Instructions

  1. Wash and dry your beard thoroughly
  2. Take a small amount of balm (about the size of a pea)
  3. Rub between your palms to warm the product
  4. Apply evenly throughout your beard
  5. Style as desired

Safety Notes

⚠️ Important Safety Information:

  • For external use only
  • Keep away from eyes and mucous membranes
  • Discontinue use if irritation occurs
  • Store in a cool, dry place
