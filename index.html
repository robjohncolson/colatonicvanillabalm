Layering Tips

For the best scent experience:

  1. Apply Cola-Tonic first to create the bright, citrus-spice foundation
  2. Wait 1-2 minutes for the top notes to settle
  3. Apply Vanilla Beard Balm to add depth and sweetness
  4. Style your beard as desired

Complete Recipe Guide

Cola-Tonic Recipe

Frankincense Resin 1.7 grams
Myrrh Resin 0.8 grams
Glycerin 250 microliters (0.25 ml)
Ethanol (95% or higher) q.s. to 15 ml
Violet Leaf Absolute 14.39 microliters
Bergamot Essential Oil 300 microliters
Neroli Essential Oil 100 microliters
Coriander Essential Oil 200 microliters
Ceylon Cinnamon Essential Oil 50 microliters
Nutmeg Absolute 100 microliters
Lime Essential Oil 50 microliters
Ginger CO2 Extract 10-15 microliters
Lemon Essential Oil 50 microliters

Instructions

  1. Grind frankincense and myrrh into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle
  2. Place ground resins in a 15 ml glass bottle with glycerin
  3. Combine all essential oils and absolutes in a clean container
  4. Add 5 ml of ethanol to the essence mixture
  5. Pour mixture into the bottle with resins
  6. Top up with additional ethanol to 15 ml
  7. Steep for 24 hours, shaking occasionally
  8. Optional: Filter through fine mesh

Vanilla Beard Balm Recipe

Carnauba Wax 7 grams
Kokum Butter 7 grams
Squalane (Olive-derived) 10 grams
Vanilla Absolute 120 microliters
Frankincense Resin 1 gram (ground fine)
Myrrh Resin 0.5 gram (ground fine)
Orange Blossom Absolute 50 microliters
Tonka Bean 20% Absolute 50 microliters
Benzoin Resinoid 50 microliters
Cardamom CO2 Extract 10-15 microliters

Instructions

  1. Grind frankincense and myrrh into a fine powder
  2. Melt carnauba wax, kokum butter, and squalane in a double boiler
  3. Add ground resins and all essences to the melted base
  4. Mix thoroughly
  5. Pour into a 30 ml tin
  6. Cool at room temperature until solid (1-2 hours)

Safety Guidelines

  • For external use only
  • Keep away from eyes and mucous membranes
  • Discontinue use if irritation occurs
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Perform a patch test before full use
